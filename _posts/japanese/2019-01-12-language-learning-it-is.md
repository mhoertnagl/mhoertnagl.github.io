As an abortive and inefficient language learning veteran I will strive to 
improve my approach to such an endeavor.
My first improvement is this journal. And this very first post itemizes the
various tactics and strategis I will exert.

## Mnemonics & Imagery
Rote memorization is a bad thing to do. Long term recall failure by far 
outweighs short term successes. I won't spend time learning new words that I 
cannot remember the week after.
Fortunately we have many more superior memoization techniquies at our disposal:
active, meaningful and associative learning.
